## 📊 Projects Overview

### 1. DASH Project (Business KPI Dashboard)

**Objective:**
Provide a high-level overview of business performance through key metrics and trends.

**What I Did:**

* Designed a clean, executive-level dashboard for quick decision-making
* Built KPI measures using DAX
* Implemented time-based analysis for trend evaluation

**Key Insights Delivered:**

* Identification of performance trends over time
* Quick visibility into core business metrics

**Value:**
Helps stakeholders monitor performance and make fast, data-driven decisions.

---

### 2. Watch Dashboard (Sales & Product Analysis)

**Objective:**
Analyze product performance and pricing strategies in the watch market.

**What I Did:**

* Developed interactive visuals to explore sales by category and brand
* Created pricing distribution analysis
* Enabled drill-down capabilities for deeper insights

**Key Insights Delivered:**

* Best-performing categories and brands
* Pricing patterns and potential optimization areas

**Value:**
Supports business decisions related to inventory management and pricing strategies.

---

### 3. Car Dashboard (Market & Performance Analysis)

**Objective:**
Provide a comprehensive view of automotive data including pricing and performance metrics.

**What I Did:**

* Built comparison tools for vehicle analysis
* Modeled data to support multi-dimensional filtering
* Designed visuals to highlight market trends

**Key Insights Delivered:**

* Comparison across vehicle attributes (price, performance, etc.)
* Market trends and segmentation

**Value:**
Enables users to evaluate products and understand market dynamics effectively.
